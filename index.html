    SINPE Móvil Configuration

    Enter your SINPE Móvil registered phone number and select your bank. Customers will see a QR code and a tap-to-pay SMS button in their cart that auto-fills the correct SMS format for instant payment.

    📱 How it works

    When a customer has items in their cart, they see:
    • A QR code they can scan with their phone
    • A "Pay via SMS" button that opens their SMS app pre-filled with:
    PASE [amount] [your_number] → sent to your bank's short code

    The SMS format (PASE) is the official SINPE Móvil SMS command accepted by all major Costa Rican banks.
    Daily SMS limit: ₡100,000 (BCCR regulation as of 2025). Higher amounts require the bank's app.
